Transforming Landscapes, One Yard at a Time

At Aquarian, our passion for landscaping and irrigation stems from the belief that every outdoor space can become a beautiful sanctuary. Over the years we have grown into a leading landscaping company in Kamloops, driven by innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our certified experts bring extensive knowledge and experience to every project, transforming ordinary yards into extraordinary landscapes. We provide comprehensive solutions tailored to our clients' unique needs, from design and installation to maintenance. Our advanced irrigation systems ensure efficient water management, keeping your garden lush year-round while maintaining eco-friendly practices. We pride ourselves on building lasting relationships with our clients, and our many happy customers are a testament to our commitment to quality and reliability.

Our skilled technicians quickly diagnose and resolve irrigation issues, ensuring your system operates efficiently and effectively.
